# Hamilton Medical Playbook

**Status:** MVP-019 playbook  
**Scope:** enrichment guidance for Hamilton Medical products

## Official Sites

- Main site: `https://www.hamilton-medical.com`
- Product pages: Hamilton Medical product sections by device family
- Documentation areas: manuals, brochures, accessories, software and support resources when publicly available

## Where To Look For Documents

- product detail pages;
- downloads or resources sections;
- operator manuals and quick guides;
- accessories and consumables documentation;
- regulator records for local registration data.

## RU Notes

- RU may list model family, configuration or variant rather than only the short commercial model;
- transport and ICU ventilators can have accessories and software options that require separate review;
- registration wording must be matched against exact model and variant.

## Typical Problems

- model family names may be written with or without `HAMILTON-`;
- accessories and consumables may appear on separate pages;
- ventilation modes can depend on software/options;
- manuals may be version-specific.

## Commonly Missing

- exact software option list;
- full accessories list;
- service manual;
- local registration mapping for all variants.

## Importer Recommendations

- normalize aliases but preserve original observed names;
- keep software/version context for modes and compatibility;
- treat accessory compatibility as review-only;
- prefer operator manual and regulator record over brochure;
- never infer compatibility from device family alone.

## Safety

Do not publish ventilation modes, alarms, accessories or compatibility until a
reviewer confirms the exact source and model/version context.
