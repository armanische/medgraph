# Dräger Playbook

**Status:** MVP-019 playbook  
**Scope:** enrichment guidance for Dräger medical products

## Official Sites

- Main site: `https://www.draeger.com`
- Medical product pages: ventilation, anesthesia, neonatal care, monitoring and lighting categories
- Download areas: IFU, product information, technical data and accessories where available

## Where To Look For Documents

- product detail pages;
- IFU/user manual downloads;
- technical data sheets;
- accessory and consumable lists;
- service-related documents when public;
- regulator records.

## RU Notes

- product names may appear with umlaut or transliterated spelling;
- model generations and variants must be preserved;
- anesthesia and neonatal products often have configuration-specific options.

## Typical Problems

- global pages may redirect by region;
- product family pages can mix multiple configurations;
- accessories and consumables are often separate from base device;
- some service documentation may not be public.

## Commonly Missing

- exact accessory matrix;
- service manual;
- local variant mapping;
- complete software/options list.

## Importer Recommendations

- preserve original brand spelling and normalized alias;
- separate base product, accessories and consumables;
- prefer IFU/manual for warnings and intended use;
- keep document language and version;
- flag configuration-dependent claims for review.

## Safety

Do not publish anesthesia compatibility, neonatal safety statements, ventilation
modes or accessory claims without official evidence and human review.
