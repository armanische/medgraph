# Ambu Playbook

**Status:** MVP-019 playbook  
**Scope:** enrichment guidance for Ambu products

## Official Sites

- Main site: `https://www.ambu.com`
- IFU portal: `https://ifu.ambu.com`
- Product pages: endoscopy, anesthesia and patient monitoring categories

## Where To Look For Documents

- IFU portal by product name or article;
- product detail pages;
- instructions for use;
- datasheets;
- regulator records;
- compatibility information for monitors and accessories.

## RU Notes

- disposable endoscopes may be represented by family, size or article;
- accessories and display units may have separate registration and IFU context;
- single-use status must come from IFU or official labeling.

## Typical Problems

- many products have similar names and generations;
- article numbers can be critical for identity;
- compatibility with displays/processors may be version-specific;
- IFU language/version matters.

## Commonly Missing

- full article mapping;
- local registration mapping for variants;
- display/accessory compatibility table;
- reprocessing details are not relevant for single-use devices but must not be guessed.

## Importer Recommendations

- prioritize IFU portal results;
- preserve article/SKU when available;
- distinguish endoscope from display/monitor product;
- mark compatibility as review-only;
- never infer single-use status from category name alone.

## Safety

Do not publish single-use, sterility, intended use or compatibility statements
without IFU-backed evidence and reviewer approval.
