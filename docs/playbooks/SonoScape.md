# SonoScape Playbook

**Status:** MVP-019 playbook  
**Scope:** enrichment guidance for SonoScape products

## Official Sites

- Main site: `https://www.sonoscape.com`
- Product pages: ultrasound and endoscopy product categories

## Where To Look For Documents

- product detail pages;
- brochure and specification downloads;
- manual or IFU links when available;
- probe and accessory information;
- regulator records for local registration.

## RU Notes

- ultrasound systems may have broad model families and software/probe options;
- endoscopy systems may require processor, scope and monitor context;
- registration record may not list all commercial options.

## Typical Problems

- brochures may be easier to find than IFU;
- probe compatibility may be incomplete on product pages;
- product naming can vary by region;
- endoscopy system components may be mixed in one page.

## Commonly Missing

- IFU/user manual;
- full probe compatibility;
- software package list;
- service manual.

## Importer Recommendations

- treat brochure as discovery and secondary evidence;
- require IFU/manual or regulator source for intended use;
- keep probe compatibility separate from base ultrasound unit;
- flag missing IFU as a quality gate blocker;
- preserve regional page source.

## Safety

Do not publish clinical applications, probe compatibility or endoscopy component
compatibility from brochure alone.
